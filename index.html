Apex High Track/XC Home Page

Welcome to the Apex High Track and Cross Country Homepage! This site allows students and parents to learn about the Apex High Cross Country, Winter Track, and Spring Track teams, sign up for the teams, and use the Race Calculator to figure out what mile pace they need to run for a certain race. Students and parents can visit the Calendar page, which has times and locations for upcoming races, or the Staff Page, to learn about the faculty involved with the teams.
